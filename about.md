A man of many hats, the artist behind the soulful project is a well rounded artist and scientist, with hands in as many disciplines as the music is allowed to be influenced by. 

Independently studying the electronic production of music since 2019, his roots can be traced back to time in the womb. Everything from Tejano to Funk, Country to R&B, HipHop to Metal, and beyond has crossed the headphones of SoulSeesBest, better known as WriterJoshua, or Joshua Lucero. 

SoulSeesBest, as a passion project, was borne out of a response to the artist’s visual challenges, and in the overcoming of them. Losing sight to the degenerative eye disease, keratoconus, inspired the naturally gifted athlete and scholar to embrace the circumstance and continue to be a creative force in the worlds of creative writing, photography, pottery, painting, cooking, and music production.

The IDM (Intelligent Dance Music) Electronica elements can be traced back to many different influences. Sometimes, one must go track by track to fully understand them all, however, some of the more prominent inspiration throughout includes: Military Drums, Trap and Southern Rap, R&B and Soul, Alternative Rock and Metal, World Music, Funk, Video Game Score, Cinematic Soundtracks, Old Hollywood, Golden Oldies, and Classic Television.
